Word Count Visualizer

Description

This is a word count visualizer which tells you the frequency of each word present in the sample text given by the user in form of a table. It also plots a graph between words and their frequency to give you a better visual representation , it also compares this plot from Zipf's law plot.


For all those who doesn't know Zipf's law - Zipf's law was originally formulated in terms of quantitative linguistics, stating that given some corpus of natural language utterances, the frequency of any word is inversely proportional to its rank in the frequency table. Thus the most frequent word will occur approximately twice as often as the second most frequent word, three times as often as the third most frequent word, etc.: the rank-frequency distribution is an inverse relation.


Thus if you'll input a natural text then the deviation will be less from zipfs's law but if you'll input text generated from sites like Lorem Ipsum , the deviation will be more as it is not a natural text.
